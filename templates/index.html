⚠️ AI Limitations

chickenegg uses AI vision to identify objects and generate repair instructions. AI can make mistakes. Always use your own judgment. Stop if something looks wrong.

🚫 Do NOT use for
  • Electrical panel or live wire work
  • Gas line installation or repair
  • Load-bearing structural work
  • Any work requiring a licensed professional
🛡️ How we protect you

AI checks your progress photos at each step. 3 danger warnings locks the task for your safety and shows emergency contacts.

📋 Your responsibility
