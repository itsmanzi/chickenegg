⚠️ AI Limitations

chickenegg uses AI to identify objects and generate repair instructions. AI can make mistakes. Always use your own judgment and common sense. If something doesn't look right, stop.

🚫 Do NOT use this app for
  • Electrical panel work or live wiring
  • Gas line installation or repair
  • Load-bearing structural work
  • Any work requiring a licensed professional by law
🛡️ How we protect you

Our AI will check your progress photos at each step and warn you if something looks dangerous. If 3 danger warnings are triggered, the task locks automatically for your safety.
